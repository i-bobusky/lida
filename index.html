About

Dr. Ludmila Bičanová

MVDr. · Dip. ECVIM-CA (Onc) · MSc (Clin Onc) · PhD · MRCVS

MVDr. Ludmila Bičanová is an EBVS® European Veterinary Specialist in both Veterinary Oncology and Radiation Oncology, and a Diplomate of the European College of Veterinary Internal Medicine – Companion Animals (ECVIM-CA). She also holds RCVS recognition as a Recognised Specialist in Veterinary Oncology.

As Medical Director at AniCura AOI – Animal Oncology and Imaging Center in Hünenberg, Switzerland, she leads a multidisciplinary team at one of Europe's premier veterinary oncology centres, combining state-of-the-art diagnostic imaging with comprehensive cancer treatment.

With over 15 years of clinical experience spanning the Czech Republic, Austria, and Switzerland, Dr. Bičanová is dedicated to providing the highest standard of specialist care — guiding families with clarity and compassion through every stage of their pet's cancer journey.

Expertise

Areas of Specialisation

Comprehensive oncological care combining medical and radiation expertise

01

Medical Oncology

Diagnosis, staging, and medical management of cancer in companion animals — including chemotherapy, immunotherapy and targeted therapies for a wide range of tumour types.

02

Radiation Oncology

Specialist consultation and planning for radiation therapy, including curative and palliative protocols, in close collaboration with referring veterinarians and radiation centres across Europe.

03

Lymphoma Diagnostics

Expert diagnosis and staging of canine and feline lymphoma, with focus on advanced modalities including flow cytometry and molecular profiling.

04

Diagnostic Oncology

Integration of advanced imaging (CT, MRI, ultrasound) with cytology and histopathology for precise tumour diagnosis, staging, and treatment planning.
